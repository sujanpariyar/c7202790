About Khokana

Khokana is tipical newari village, About 4 kilometers from Kathmandu city limit (ring road) to the south in the lalitpur district lies the village of Khokana. The village represents the richness of the early Malla era (13th century). About 4927 indigenous people inhabit in Khokana. The male and female ratio is 28:31 and 22:21.
